INTRODUCTION

Motivated by the history of Kenyan elections, we felt compelled to reform the system for the better.
The Kenyan government has been suggesting digitizing the voting system for some time, and as a software developer, I have created a prototype that resembles the actual elections.
The approach is aimed at lowering and avoiding altogether the nullification of votes,getting away from the paper voting system, and beefing up the security of the voting system.
