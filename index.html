Introduction

Motivated by the history of Kenyan elections, we felt compelled to reform the system for the better. The Kenyan government has been suggesting digitizing the voting system for some time, and as software developers, we have created a prototype that resembles actual elections. The approach is aimed at reducing and avoiding altogether the nullification of votes, moving away from the paper voting system, and strengthening the security of the voting system.

Many African countries have poor and outdated voting systems - the old pen and paper method! This has brought a lot of compromise not only in Kenya but in all African Nations. With people lacking trust in government as a whole, one way to restore trust is through reformation of the electoral commissions in all countries.
